SMS Privacy Policy

1. Collection of Information

When you opt into our SMS campaigns, we collect your phone number and any additional information you provide as part of the opt-in process.

2. Use of Information

We use your phone number and consent information solely for the purposes of sending SMS messages related to the campaign you opted into.

3. Sharing of Information

No Unauthorized Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your SMS opt-in data with third parties for marketing or unrelated purposes.

Service Providers: We may share your SMS opt-in status with third-party service providers that help facilitate our messaging services, such as:
  • SMS platform providers

  • Mobile carriers and network operators

  • Technology vendors assisting in message delivery
4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure data storage practices.

5. User Rights and Opt-Out Policy

Opting Out: You can opt out of our SMS campaigns at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Data Requests: You may request access, correction, or deletion of your SMS-related data by contacting us at [your contact email].

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and we will notify users where required by law.
